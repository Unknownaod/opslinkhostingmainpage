About OpsLink Hosting

OpsLink Hosting is part of the OpsLink Systems network — providing reliable infrastructure for game servers, Discord bots, and enterprise dispatch solutions since 2024.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: deliver enterprise-grade performance to small and mid-sized communities. Whether you're running a FiveM city, a GMod sandbox, or a large voice network — OpsLink Hosting offers the tools and uptime you need to grow, without overspending on “slot-based” pricing.

Infrastructure

Every OpsLink node runs on NVMe-backed systems with automatic DDoS mitigation and redundant uplinks. We deploy through Google Cloud and OVH networks for consistent latency and stability. Our control layer is powered by Pterodactyl, giving clients full transparency and access to their environments.

Part of OpsLink Systems

OpsLink Hosting operates under the OpsLink Systems umbrella — the same group behind OpsLink CAD, OpsLink PremierOne, and several next-gen roleplay technology solutions. Together, we’re building a unified ecosystem of tools for communities that value professionalism and realism.
